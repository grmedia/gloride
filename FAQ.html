Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about Greenify's features, data, and sustainability.

General Questions

What is Greenify and how does it work?

Greenify is a web application designed to help you track and reduce your environmental footprint. It works by allowing you to log your daily activities (like transportation, energy use, and consumption), calculating your estimated carbon emissions, and providing personalized tips and challenges to help you live more sustainably.

Is Greenify free to use?

Greenify offers a comprehensive free tier with most of its core features. We also have premium features (e.g., advanced analytics, exclusive challenges) available through an optional subscription to support our ongoing development.

How does Greenify calculate my carbon footprint?

We use a combination of scientific data, publicly available emission factors, and advanced AI algorithms to estimate your carbon footprint based on the data you provide about your activities. Our system continuously learns and refines its calculations for accuracy.

Data & Privacy

Is my data safe and private with Greenify?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption for all data, never share your personal identifiable information with third parties without your explicit consent, and comply with global data protection regulations like GDPR and CCPA. You can manage your data preferences in the Settings section.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your personal data and carbon tracking history as a CSV file from your Settings page under the "Data & Privacy" section.

Features & Usage

How do I connect my smart home devices?

You can connect compatible smart home devices (like smart thermostats or energy monitors) via the "Smart Home Integration" option in your Settings. Follow the on-screen prompts to authorize the connection securely.

What are Eco-Challenges and how do I join?

Eco-Challenges are fun, goal-oriented activities designed to help you adopt greener habits. You can browse and join challenges from the "Challenges" section in the app. Some challenges are personalized based on your activity data!

Can I chat with an AI assistant for eco-tips?

Yes! Our AI-powered Eco-Assistant is available 24/7 to answer your sustainability questions and provide instant tips. Just click on the "Chatbot" link in the navigation menu or visit the "Help & Support" section in Settings.

Support & Contact

How can I contact Greenify support?

You can email our support team at support@greenify.com, or use the "Report a Bug" link in the Settings page if you encounter an issue.
